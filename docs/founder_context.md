## Business Vision: Sole Trader Consultancy

*   **Structure**: Sole Trader (Individual Consultant).
*   **Target Market**: Small and Medium Businesses (SMBs) needing operational efficiency, systems automation, or growth consulting.
*   **Operating Model**:
    *   Side-gig running during **evenings and weekends**.
    *   **Low-overhead / high-margin**: Offering end-to-end strategic design + direct software construction (vibe coding) which typically required hiring full developer teams in the past.
    *   **Low Cost Entry**: Starting with competitive, lower rates to secure early case studies and testimonials, then scaling up.

## Operating Preferences & System Automation

*   **Minimizing Administrative Drag**: Because this business runs on evenings/weekends, the founder must avoid being bogged down by administration (onboarding, contract writing, invoice chasing).
*   **Agent-Assisted Management**: Leverage AI agents running on the **free tier** (specifically Google AI Studio) to assist with drafting, invoicing, project tracking, and client logs.
*   **Infrastructure Strategy**: Build automated workflows using GitHub Actions and free tools so the founder can trigger agent actions from their phone even when their laptop is shut.
*   **Solution Standardization**: Solve common client problems by developing reusable script templates (marketing trackers, data integration blocks) to drastically reduce turnaround times.
