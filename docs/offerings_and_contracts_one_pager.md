# Services & Contractual Approaches One-Pager

## 1. Core Value Proposition
We bridge the gap between **high-level business strategy** and **direct technical execution**. 
*   **The Problem**: Traditional agencies charge high fees for overhead, and standard developers often lack strategic business/marketing context.
*   **Our Solution**: A single, senior growth consultant (former Deputy Director of Growth) who understands business unit economics, strategy, and marketing-technology, and directly builds the automations, pipelines, and integrations (via high-speed vibe coding). 
*   **Result**: Faster turnaround, zero communication lag, and significantly lower project costs for SMBs.

## 2. Strategic Solutions Menu
What we offer, categorized by business impact:

### A. Marketing Technology & Attribution
*   **True ROI Tracker**: Automated pipelines that pull ad spend and conversion data from Meta, Google Ads, and TikTok APIs into Google Sheets or PostgreSQL to calculate real-time Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) and Lifetime Value (LTV).
*   **Conversion API (CAPI) Integrations**: Custom server-side event tracking setups to bypass browser ad-blockers and feed accurate conversion signals back to advertising platforms.

### B. Systems & CRM Integrations
*   **CRM Data Sync & Enrichment**: Webhook-based pipelines that catch incoming leads, enrich them using third-party lookup APIs, and automatically populate Salesforce or HubSpot.
*   **Lead Routing & Slack Alerts**: Real-time sales triggers that ping Slack when high-intent prospects perform actions (e.g., visiting the pricing page, downloading a whitepaper).

### C. Automated Data Engineering & Reporting
*   **Scheduled KPI Digests**: Lightweight serverless scripts that query production databases daily or weekly, generating automated PDF executive summaries sent via Email or Slack.
*   **Data Lakehouse Setup**: Simple data warehousing solutions (e.g. BigQuery, Snowflake, or localized DuckDB/PostgreSQL instances) aggregating data for internal analytics.
